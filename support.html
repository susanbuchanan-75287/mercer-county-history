Preserve the past

Become a member.

When you join, you become an active steward of Mercer County's irreplaceable heritage. Your support maintains our museums, funds research and hosts educational programs — we're sustained entirely by members and volunteers.

Why join

Your membership makes it possible.

🎟 Free museum access

Complimentary admission to both the War Museum and the Agricultural Museum, all year.

📰 Stay informed

Our exclusive newsletter details new acquisitions, discoveries and upcoming events.

⭐ Priority access

Priority registration and discounted tickets for our popular lectures and tours.

🗳 Voting rights

Participate in the annual meeting and help elect the Board of Directors.
