Open to the public

Events & programs.

Throughout the year we host a monthly speaker series, historic walking tours and hands-on workshops. All events are open to the public unless otherwise noted — members enjoy priority seating and discounts.

Recurring programs

What we host.

Dates rotate through the year — call (304) 425-9017 or follow us on Facebook for the next scheduled date and to reserve a seat.

Monthly

Speaker Series

Talks on Mercer County history — from early industries and coal camps to agriculture — usually at the Agricultural Museum.

Free for members · small fee for non-members
Seasonal

Historic Walking Tours

Guided walks through downtown Princeton architecture and the historic districts, led by MCHS volunteers.

Meet downtown · modest per-person fee
Hands-on

Archiving Workshops

Practical sessions such as caring for family photographs and preserving documents — materials included.

At the MCHS Research Center
Annual

Civil War Lecture Series

Our signature lecture exploring the Civil War in the region, with priority seating for MCHS members.

Contact us for the next date
